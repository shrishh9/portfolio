Carbon FootPrint Tracking and carbon emission prediction

  • Tech Stack: Python, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, GCP(Pub/Sub, Dataflow, Data Studio, GAE, BigQuery)
  • Github URL: Project Link

first phase uses a Carbon Footprint Prediction Model, which has the capacity to estimate them up to the next 6 years. The predictive model will utilize techniques like autoregressive integrated moving average, seasonal autoregressive integrated moving average, simple exponential smoothing autoregression, Holt-Winters models for nonstationary data, and generate a forecast against trends in historical emissions data. Arima has given the highest accuracy of 99.2% followed by Sarimax and Holt-Winters with 99.02% and 98.73% accuracy respectively.

second phase proves to be very innovative as it is an interactive platform where individuals can calculate their carbon emissions with some real time data collected. The chatbot asks the user for real-time data related to his or her habits—transportation, energy usage, eating habits, and other such things—computes a person's carbon footprint, and gives insights into it. Further, it will also give practical tips and recommendations on how one can reduce carbon emissions so he or she can live a more sustainable life.
